Why I Chose Multiverse

As a self-taught learner I was very interested in joining an apprenticeship scheme with a great company with an amazing training provider to ensure I get the most out of my apprenticeship.

After many days of searching indeed.com and total jobs I ended up on Multiverse which was completely different but in a good way, they offered me a platform to showcase myself as a developer and allow employers to take me seriously. Lastly now that I got an apprenticeship and Multiverse are my training provider I can say that the future is bright.

Why I Chose Mindshare

At the time I was searching for an apprenticeship, I had applied for about 15 different companies as I was very eager to start one, however after some thought I realised not all apprenticeships were the same so I did research on all the companies and what they do, also looking at the 'typical day' of the apprenticeship.

What I found was that many companies that wrote Software Engineer L4 wasn't the same as other companies so that was when I starting paying a lot of attention to what software and practices companies used and thats where I fell in love with mindshare as they used a lot of things I used and enjoyed to use, therefore I knew Mindshare was for me and now that I am there I don't regret choosing Mindshare.
